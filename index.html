About Us

Who we are & what we do.

The Acadia Medical Campus Response Team (AMCRT) is a team of student Volunteer Medical Responders who provide first aid at major events on Acadia’s campus and in the surrounding community of Wolfville. AMCRT is proud to work in collaboration with the Acadia Students’ Union, Acadia Safety and Security, Acadia Residence Life and St. John Ambulance, an international not-for-profit first aid organization. Equipped with advanced medical first responder training, AMCRT members strive toward a greater sense of community as well as the upmost safety and wellbeing of students, staff and faculty, and community members. We are available to cover any events on campus or in the Wolfville community.
